UrlCut Application

UrlCut is a URL utility application designed to automatically extract and clean tracking parameters and marketing tokens from shared URLs, persisting them in a local lightweight database for organized management and one-click sharing.

Key Features

✨ URL Cleaner Utility

  • Tracking Parameters Removal: Automatically scans and removes bloated query parameters like utm_source, fbclid, gclid, NaPm, etc.
  • Preservation of Critical Identifiers: Retains necessary store item IDs, article IDs, and structural variables required for successful web page loading.

📥 Share Intent Detection

  • Seamless Integration: Simply click the native share sheet in browsers, shopping portals, or video apps, and choose UrlCut to parse and clean your URL instantly.
  • Background Support: Supports receiving and resolving share intents in both foreground and background application states.

💾 Hive Local Storage and Organization

  • Lightweight Local Database: Utilizes Hive for high-speed local storage, ensuring your links stay saved only on your device.
  • Title Customization: Enables editing saved URL metadata with intuitive, single-line titles to keep your collection legible.
  • Folder Grouping: Organizes links into user-defined folders (e.g., Shopping, Work, Reading list) to simplify retrieval.

🔗 Quick Actions

  • One-Click Sharing: Integrated with share_plus to launch the native sharing dialog to quickly send cleaned URLs via chat or mail apps.
  • Clipboard Copying: Copy refined, clean URLs directly to the clipboard with one simple tap.

📱 Instagram Media Downloader

  • Media Downloading: Conveniently fetches media from Instagram link items.
  • Platform-specific Storage: Saves media directly to the Photos Album (PhotoKit) on iOS devices, and writes files directly to the Downloads folder on macOS environments.
